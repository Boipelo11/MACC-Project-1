Our Founder


Thandoluhle Vilakazi

Thandoluhle fell pregnant in 2017 at the age of 20 while being a student in University. She took note of the continuous tremendous support she received from friends and family throughout her pregnancy and decided she wanted to grant other young mothers the opportunity to receive support and be a voice for those who have been silenced.

She envisions a future where young mothers are provided with holistic support systems that address their spiritual, physical, emotional, and psychological well-being. A society that values and invests in the potential of every young mother, offering mentorship, vocational training, and entrepreneurship programs to foster economic independence. By cultivating their leadership abilities and self-confidence, her mandate is to empower young mothers and young women to become agents of change, influencing policy and advocating for the rights of young parents everywhere.

South Africa has one of the highest rates of teen pregnancy in the world, with nearly 25% of girls falling pregnant before turning 20 years old

33% of girls do not return to school after falling pregnant. The lack of education diminishes opportunities for advancement available to teen mothers, perpetuating the inequality and poverty that made them vulnerable in the first place.

In 2022, 47% of women were recorded as economically inactive. Women that find employment are often in lowing paying jobs, where improvement prospects are limited.

60% of South African children have absent fathers and more than 40% of South African mothers are single parents.



How You Can Help

Your support and involvement are crucial in driving our foundation's vision forward. There are various ways you can contribute to our cause and make a meaningful difference. Volunteering your time and skills is a powerful way to get directly involved. Whether it's organizing events, mentoring individuals, or assisting in our day-to-day operations, your dedication can amplify our efforts and create lasting positive outcomes.

Making donations, even the smallest amount can create significant change. Your generosity enables us to initiate and sustain vital programs that uplift the young mothers that are in need. Furthermore, spreading awareness about our and its initiatives through your social networks and personal connections helps us reach a wider audience and garner greater support. By sharing our vision, you inspire others to join our cause and collectively bring about transformative change.
